About me.

My name is Samuel, I am originally from the UK. I am an aspiring web developer and front end engineer living in Meguro, Tokyo. I have been living in Japan for over ten years with most of that being in Tokyo working as and English teacher and IT support. After Tokyo I moved to Hokkaido for 3 years where I worked at an outdoor apparel and equipment store near to the local ski resorts. Here I completely fell in love with the snow of Hokkaido, snowboarding and the nature of Japan, spending a lot of time hiking the nearby mountains during the summer. As a consequence my personal websites all seem to end up with a snowboarding theme on them. When I'm not snowboarding or playing video games I am perhaps ill advisedly trying to learn how to build a synthesiser in JavaScript. An endeaver which is apparently as much about learning the bottomless pit that is sound engineering as it is about programming.
